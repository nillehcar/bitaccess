About BitAccess

BitAccess Inc. produces the world's leading Bitcoin Automatic Teller Machine (BTM). Our machines allow anyone to deposit cash and instantly receive a digital wallet containing the equivalent in the Bitcoin cryptocurrency, or do the reverse and exchange Bitcoin for traditional currency. Currently, BitAccess already has installed Bitcoin ATMs in half a dozen countries, with dozens more launching in the next few months. Founded in November 2013, BitAccess is a privately held company headquartered in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. BitAccess's mission is to provide people and businesses around the world with access to the future digital money technology. We promise to develop technologies that are secure, accessible and easy-to-use.
